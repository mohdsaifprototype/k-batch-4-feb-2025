Agra

Agra is a city on the southern bank of the Yamuna River in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is located about 200 km southwest of the national capital New Delhi. Agra is one of the most populous cities in Uttar Pradesh and the 24th most populous city in India. Agra is home to the Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the Modern World. The city is also known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and historical landmarks such as the Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri.
