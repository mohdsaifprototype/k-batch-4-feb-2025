Kashmir

Kashmir is a region located in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent. It is a disputed territory, claimed by India and Pakistan, and divided between them since 1947. The Indian-administered part of Kashmir is referred to as Jammu and Kashmir, while the Pakistani-administered part is referred to as Azad Kashmir and Gilgit Baltistan. The region is known for its natural beauty, with the Himalayan mountains and the Kashmir Valley being popular tourist destinations.
